Share log - Rescapp - Documentation
  • Step 1

    Rescapp searches for log files that have been generated by other Rescapp options.
  • Step 2

    You are displayed the list of files found:

    Please choose a log file

  • Step 3

    Rescapp sends the log file contents to a public pastebin service (originally paste.debian.net) and gathers the associated url.
  • Step 4

    You are prompted with:

    The url

    And also asked to: Right-click and select copy. You will be able to paste it later in chat.

  • Step 5

    You are supposed to go manually go to the Chat option (originally under Support menu), and paste the copied url if you want help. But please be sure that someone is willing to help you before doing it.
